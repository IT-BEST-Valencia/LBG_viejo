Students

Thanks to our events, students can develop their technical and soft skills. Moreover, our international environment help the students to understand the cultural diversity of Europe and learn how to overcome our little differences.

Companies

We offer a professional infrastructure for technology students giving an overview of their career. To do this, we get the students in contact with local and international companies that offer them the possibility of being better prepared for their professional future.

University

BEST Valencia organizes events and meetings where students are given the chance to analyze, discuss, and express their view to teachers and proffesionals about the latest teaching methods in engineering with the goal to improve the local, national or even european educational system.

Valencian Innovative
Engineering Week

VIEW was born in 2014 with the goal of getting the students closer to the innovation in the engineering and technology field, as well as inspire them to participate. Different conferences and activities are held during the week along with an engineering competition with two different categories designed to challenge the students and prove their organization and teamwork skills.

Find out more about VIEW


Spring Course

BEST provides short duration courses that allow students to develop in their own field of studies or in specific skills that will be useful for their professional future. At BEST Valencia we arrange a course for 22 european students that come to Valencia to enrich their university experience.

Find out more about the course


Hackathon

The Hackathon is an event whose main activity corresponds to a programming competition in which the problem raised by the VIEWNext company of the IBM group will have to be solved by developing a tool to help solve it. A pre-competition class will be given so that all participants start from the same knowledge base on the subject and the way to solve the proposed problem. If you want to test yourself, this is your event !!
